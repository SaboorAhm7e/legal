Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of our mobile applications for iOS devices. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe user experience.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our applications are designed to respect user privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers. Any processing of data (such as camera input, microphone audio, text, or speech recognition) happens entirely on your physical device locally, or is securely handled via native Apple frameworks.

2. Device Permissions

To provide core functionalities, our apps may request access to certain hardware features on your device. These may include:

  • Camera: Used strictly for features requiring visual input, such as scanning and image recognition.
  • Microphone: Used to capture live audio for real-time processing or voice capabilities.
  • Speech Recognition: Used to convert spoken utterances into text via native system APIs.

You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time through your device's native Settings application.

3. Third-Party Services

Our applications utilize native Apple infrastructure services (such as StoreKit for purchases and iCloud for syncing). These services are governed by Apple's standard privacy guidelines. We do not integrate data-harvesting trackers or sell your information to third-party advertising companies.

4. Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect or solicit data from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided any data through our platform, please contact us so we can investigate and address it.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly to this web page, and your continued use of the applications constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using our applications, please reach out to us via our GitHub support channel.
